What it does

Stop guessing your win rate — know it, matchup by matchup. ManaMetrics lets you log every match in seconds with tappable tags, then dig into analytics that reveal exactly where you win and where you lose.

  • Tag-based logging: format, deck, opponent, venue, event, and play/draw in a few taps
  • Stackable filters to answer specific matchup questions, with pinned favorites
  • Per-deck win rates and head-to-head opponent profiles
  • Built-in life tracker with dice roll and coin flip
  • Voice and text notes on any match, plus full local backup export/import
